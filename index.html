About Me

I am a Full-Stack Developer with a background in pharmacy technology. My experience as a pharmacy technician has provided me with valuable transferable skills that are useful in the tech industry. For instance, I have developed exceptional customer service skills, a keen attention to detail, and the ability to manage a high volume of tasks efficiently.
With a strong foundation in JavaScript and Python, I am committed to delivering quality work that meets the needs of my team and aligns with company goals. I thrive in both collaborative and independent environments and possess excellent communication skills that allow me to connect with colleagues and customers effectively.
I am driven by a desire to constantly learn and grow, and to tackle new challenges that drive success for the company. My passion for technology and problem-solving is what drew me to software engineering, and I am excited to be a part of this fast-paced and ever-evolving industry.
Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new technologies and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in the tech industry. I also enjoy volunteering my time and expertise to mentor aspiring developers and contribute to open-source projects.
I believe that my unique background and skillset make me a valuable asset to any team, and I am excited to contribute to the success of your company.
